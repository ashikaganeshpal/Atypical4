How it works?

1.Customers Upload Designs:

    Customers upload photos of their traditional wear designs.
    They provide details such as fabric type, size, color, and preferred delivery time.

2. Tailors Browse & Select:

    Registered tailors review the submitted designs.
    If a tailor is confident in recreating the design, they approve it and submit their price, estimated delivery time, and any discounts they can offer.

3. Customer Approves & Pays:

    Once a tailor selects a design, the customer is notified with the final price, delivery time, and discount.
    The customer can then proceed to place the order.
    Payment is made online at the time of order placement (no cash on delivery).

4. Secure Payment & Order Fulfillment:

    The payment is held by us for 15-20 days while the tailor works on the order.
    After successful delivery, the payment is released to the tailor.
    This model ensures financial security while allowing us to generate additional revenue by utilizing the held funds.

5. No Returns or Exchanges:

    Since each piece is custom-made, there is no return or exchange policy, ensuring commitment from both parties.
